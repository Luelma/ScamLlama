Scam Llama Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Our Commitment

Scam Llama is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe you should be able to check for scams without giving up your personal information.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. Scam Llama has no backend servers, user accounts, or analytics services.

How Analysis Works

  • Local Pattern Scanning: Text analysis against 300+ scam patterns happens entirely on your device. No data leaves your phone.
  • AI Analysis (Optional): If you provide your own Claude API key, conversation text is sent directly to Anthropic's API (api.anthropic.com) for enhanced analysis. Only the conversation text is sent — no personal identifiers, contact names, or device information. This requires your explicit consent.
  • Photo/Video/Audio Verification (Optional): Media files are sent to Reality Defender and/or Scam.ai for deepfake detection. Only the media file is transmitted — no metadata or personal information. This requires your explicit consent.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Analysis History: Past analyses are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. This data never leaves your phone.
  • API Keys: Your API keys are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device.
  • Checklist Sessions: Your checklist responses are stored locally.

Third-Party Services

When you opt in, Scam Llama communicates directly with:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — For AI-powered text analysis. Governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Reality Defender — For photo, video, and audio deepfake detection.
  • Scam.ai — For photo and video deepfake detection.

No data is shared with advertising networks, analytics providers, or data brokers.

No Tracking

Scam Llama contains no analytics SDKs, no tracking pixels, no cookies, and no advertising frameworks. We don't know who you are or how you use the app.

Children's Privacy

Scam Llama does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
