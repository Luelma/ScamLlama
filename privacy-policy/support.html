Frequently Asked Questions

What does Scam Llama detect?

Scam Llama scans text for 300+ patterns across 35 financial scam types including tech support scams, phishing, crypto/investment fraud, fake job offers, lottery scams, government impersonation, bank fraud, and more. It also verifies photos, videos, and audio for deepfakes.

Do I need an API key?

No. Scam Llama works offline with local pattern scanning and the interactive checklist. For AI-powered deep analysis, you can optionally add your own Anthropic Claude API key in Settings. Photo/video/audio deepfake detection works out of the box.

Is my data private?

Yes. All local analysis happens on your device. If you use AI analysis, only the text you submit is sent to Anthropic's API — no personal identifiers. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics. See our Privacy Policy.

How accurate is the detection?

Scam Llama uses pattern matching and AI analysis to identify known scam tactics. It's a tool to help you spot red flags — not a guarantee. Always use your judgment and consult trusted sources before making financial decisions.

I think I've been scammed. What should I do?

If you've sent money or shared personal information:

  • Contact your bank immediately to attempt to reverse transactions
  • Change passwords on any compromised accounts
  • Report to the FTC at reportfraud.ftc.gov
  • File a report with the FBI's IC3 at ic3.gov
  • Consider placing a credit freeze with all three credit bureaus

What's the difference between Scam Llama and Love Llama?

Love Llama focuses specifically on romance scam detection in dating contexts. Scam Llama covers all types of financial scams — phishing, investment fraud, impersonation, job scams, and more. Same great technology, different focus.
