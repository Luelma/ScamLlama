## Description
Scam Llama is your AI-powered scam detection tool. Paste a suspicious message, screenshot a sketchy text, or run through our red flag checklist — Scam Llama identifies the tactics scammers use so you can protect your money and identity.

SMART TEXT ANALYSIS
Paste any suspicious text — emails, texts, DMs, or chat messages — and Scam Llama instantly scans for 300+ known scam patterns across 35 fraud categories. Get an instant risk assessment with detailed explanations of every red flag found.

35 SCAM TYPES DETECTED
- Phishing & account verification scams
- Crypto & investment fraud
- Tech support scams
- Bank & government impersonation
- Fake job offers & work-from-home schemes
- Lottery, prize & inheritance scams
- Package delivery scams
- Rental & housing fraud
- Blackmail & extortion
- And many more

AI-POWERED DEEP ANALYSIS
Add your own Claude API key for AI-enhanced analysis that goes beyond pattern matching. Claude examines context, conversation flow, and subtle manipulation tactics that simple keyword matching can miss.

DEEPFAKE DETECTION
Verify photos, videos, and voice recordings for AI manipulation. Powered by Reality Defender and Scam.ai, our triple-check system catches AI-generated and manipulated media.

INTERACTIVE RED FLAG CHECKLIST
Work through our 41-point financial scam checklist covering initial contact red flags, pressure tactics, payment demands, information harvesting, and investment fraud indicators. Get a weighted risk score based on your specific situation.

PRIVACY FIRST
- All local analysis happens on your device
- No accounts, no tracking, no ads
- API keys stored in iOS Keychain
- AI analysis requires your explicit consent
- We never see your data

Built for anyone who wants to stay safe: seniors protecting against phone scams, investors evaluating opportunities, job seekers screening offers, or anyone who gets a suspicious message.

Don't get scammed. Let Scam Llama check it first.
